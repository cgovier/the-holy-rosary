Objective

An interdisciplinary project was developed, including the subjects of Art, Religion, and Learning Innovation. The students had the opportunity to understand the history and the prayers of the Rosary, record their own prayers, learn how to insert the audio into a website using Google Sites, and design a colorful Rosary made with pieces of wood. Besides the intention of religious connection and education, this opportunity reveals that praying can be engaging, contextualized, and up-to-date. The website is published, and everyone with the link can access and pray along!
